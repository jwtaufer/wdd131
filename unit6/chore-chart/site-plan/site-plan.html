Overview

Purpose

Chore Chart's purpose is to manage our family's household chores and divvy them out evenly between each family member each day. It will manage daily chores and weekly chores, storing them in respective queues to be divvied out when the time is right.

Audience

Its intended audience is my family, the Taufer family.

Dynamic elements

Dynamic elements will primarily include the to-do list for each person. These will be checklists that are generated from an invisible queue, and crossed out when the box is checked. A new list will be generated each day.

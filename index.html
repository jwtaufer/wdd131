Utah, United States

Utah is a beautiful desert state in the western United States of America. Surrounding it are the states of Idaho, Wyoming, Colorado, New Mexico, Arizona, and Nevada. Utah was settled by pioneers of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-days Saints, on July 24, 1847, who were seeking relief from religious persecution. Today, Utah is home to people of many different faiths and religious organizations, with the Church of Jesus Christ making up 56% of the state's population.
